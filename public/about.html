About Me

I was born on December 13, 1988 in the city of Nakhchivan, Republic of Azerbaijan.

Between 2007 and 2011, I studied economics at the Istanbul University of the Republic of Turkey.

I started my first job in 2012 as an internal auditor at Gamigaya Holding. After successfully passing the entrance exam of the State Customs Committee of the Republic of Azerbaijan in 2015, I continue my work in that Committee.

Since the beginning of 2023, I have been learning programming through online courses.

I have been married since 2017 and have two children.
